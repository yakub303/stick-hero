Stick Hero 🎮
A beautifully crafted, premium version of the classic Stick Hero game with user authentication, combo system, perfect hit mechanics, and stunning visual effects.

✨ Features
🎯 Gameplay
Classic Stick Hero Mechanics - Hold to stretch the stick, release to bridge gaps between platforms

Perfect Hit System - Land exactly in the center of platforms for bonus points

Combo System - Chain perfect hits to increase your score multiplier

Dynamic Difficulty - Platforms become more challenging as your combo grows

👤 User System
Authentication - Register and login to save your progress

Persistent High Scores - Your best score is saved to your account

User Statistics - Track games played and total score

🎨 Visual Features
Glassmorphic UI - Modern, translucent design elements

Animated Background - Floating particles and twinkling stars

Dynamic Visual Feedback - "PERFECT!" animations, combo indicators, score popups

Gradient Effects - Premium color schemes throughout

Responsive Design - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

🎮 Game Mechanics
Score System

Normal hit: +1 point

Perfect hit: +2 points + combo bonus

Combo bonus: Extra point every 3 perfect hits

Progressive Difficulty - Platform gaps adjust based on your performance

Physics-based Movement - Smooth walking, falling, and transitioning animations

🎮 How to Play
Create an Account - Register with a username and password (min. 3 chars username, 4 chars password)

Game Controls:

Desktop: Click and hold on the canvas to stretch, release to place the stick

Mobile: Touch and hold, then release

Keyboard: Press 'R' to restart after game over

Objective: Extend the stick to reach the next platform

Perfect Hit: Land the stick exactly in the red zone (center of platform) for bonus points

Build Combos: Consecutive perfect hits increase your combo multiplier

🎯 Scoring System
Hit Type	Base Points	Bonus
Normal Hit	+1	-
Perfect Hit	+2	Combo points
3+ Perfect Combo	+2	Extra +1 point
